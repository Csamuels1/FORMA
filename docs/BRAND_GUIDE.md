# FORMA Brand Guide

## Brand personality

FORMA should feel like a disciplined coach that respects privacy and effort. The voice is direct, calm, and practical. It encourages without hype and avoids shaming.

## Tone of voice

- Clear and concise
- Supportive without being coddling
- No bro-science
- No clinical distance
- No fake urgency

## Naming and tagline

- Brand name: FORMA
- Tagline: Your body. Your goal. Your plan. Every day.

## Color palette

### Primary

- Deep charcoal: `#111827`
- Graphite: `#1F2937`
- Clean white: `#F9FAFB`

### Accent

- Energy green: `#22C55E`
- Focus blue: `#2563EB`

### Semantic

- Success: `#16A34A`
- Streak: `#F97316`
- Warning: `#F59E0B`
- Error: `#DC2626`

### Reasoning

- The palette should feel grounded and strong rather than loud.
- Green signals progress and health.
- Orange supports streak and momentum.
- Blue provides a calm trust layer for analytics and system states.

## Typography

- Recommended direction: a clean sans serif family with strong readability, such as Inter or SF Pro on iOS and Roboto on Android, unless a branded typeface is chosen later.
- Use heavier weights for numbers, plans, and progress states.
- Keep body copy compact and highly legible.

## Iconography

- Use a flame motif for streaks, but keep it minimal and modern.
- Use illustrated silhouettes for goal selection.
- Avoid real body comparison imagery in marketing or UI decoration.
- Use line icons with solid fills for emphasis states.

## Logo concept direction

- A geometric wordmark with a subtle frame or angle motif that implies structure and progress.
- A secondary icon could combine a frame and an upward path shape.
- The mark should work on dark and light backgrounds and stay legible at app-icon sizes.

## Microcopy examples

### Onboarding

- "Tell us your goal. We will build the plan."
- "Home only for now. No gym needed."
- "Your photos stay private. We will show the policy before you continue."

### Streak milestones

- "7 days locked in."
- "You kept the chain alive."
- "Momentum looks good on you."

### Rest days

- "Recovery is part of the plan."
- "Today is lighter. Stay consistent."

### Empty states

- "Your feed is quiet right now."
- "No meals logged yet. Start with one entry."

### Ad placements

- "Sponsored" labels must be plain and unobtrusive.
- Ad content must never interrupt active workout or photo flows.
